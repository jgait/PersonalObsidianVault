**Linear Systems** - Linear systems are those that consist only of variables with a power of 1 (i.e. $x^1$) and are multiplied by a constant (i.e. $ax+b \dot{x}$ = c)

Examples:
$$
\ddot{x} + x = 0 \rightarrow \text{   is linear}

$$
